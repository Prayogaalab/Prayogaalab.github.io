About PrayogaaLab

PRAYOGAA LAB is an educational Laboratory based institute,It was founded in 2021 by Akash Shakya, Harshit Dubey and Akshay Sanwal that aims at providing quality education to the all aspirants and enhance the skills and develop the thought process of students. PrayogaaLab provide quality education with practical skills

Our Vision

Our vision at Prayogaa is to provide high-quality education in physics, coding, and programming to students of all ages. We aim to create a world where everyone has access to the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their academic and professional pursuits.

Our Mission

At Prayogaa, our mission is to provide comprehensive and engaging classes in physics, coding, and programming to students of all levels. We are dedicated to fostering a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. Our goal is to help students develop a deep understanding of physics and programming concepts and equip them with the skills they need to succeed in their academic and professional lives.

Our Values

We encourage collaboration and teamwork among our students, recognizing that working together can lead to greater understanding, learning, and success.
